Document translation

DeepL allows free users to translate entire documents in the following formats:

  • Word (.docs, .doc) up to 5MB or 100,000 characters per month
  • PowerPoint (.pptx) up to 5MB or 100,000 characters per month
  • PDF (.pdf) up to 5MB or 100,000 characters per month

 

Translating documents using the online version

To translate documents:

  1. Open the desktop app.
  2. Click on the Translate files tab.
  3. Drag and drop your document file into the text area.
  4. Select the target language from the drop-down list.
  5. Click on Translate into (your target language). The translation will start automatically.
